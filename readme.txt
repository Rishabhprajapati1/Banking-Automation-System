Banking Automation System:
Into: In the bank all the activities are being done manually .As the bank widens its services & it finds difficult to manage its operations manually and hence this leads to the automation of some of its operations.
       Banking Information system is a windows based applications. This project mainly deals with managing accounts and their related operations .
      Tasks involved in this project are opening the user accounts , recording the account holders transactions , depositing, withdrawing  amount.
